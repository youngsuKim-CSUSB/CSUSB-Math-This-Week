Cal-Bridge Program

Apr 1, 2023

Are you a math, math education, or statistics major interested in obtaining a PhD? Apply to the Cal-Bridge program! The deadline this year is April 11, 2023. Interested students should go to one of the following sites: Physics and Astronomy, Computer Science and Computer Engineering, Math, Math Education, and Statistics.

The Outreach and Recruiting Committee will be holding a virtual workshop about Cal-Bridge and how to apply, see this flyer

Application workshop: Saturday, April 1, 11 am - 12:30 pm PT Interested students should register for the workshop by completing Google form. Registration in advance is required to receive the Zoom connection info.
